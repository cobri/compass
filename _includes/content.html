Prior to this, I worked in a next-generation sequencing facility. I hold a BSc in Neuroscience from University College Cork and an MPhil in Computational Biology from the University of Cambridge. If you’d like to know more about my research you can check it out here.

Outside of my research, I have a deep interest in journalism and travel, so I founded Trinity College Dublin’s International Affairs and Travel magazine. I’m also passionate about STEM outreach and I write about science for my university newspaper.
